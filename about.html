THE WOMAN BEHIND THE VISION

OSENOTSE GENERAL TRADING (PTY) LTD was established in 2023 by Segomotsi Rooibaadjie – Mabilo, a visionary entrepreneur with deep-rooted construction experience in the Tsantsabane area of the Northern Cape.

Her unwavering commitment to quality and community inspired a company that is both 100% women-owned and 100% black-owned — built from the ground up to serve and empower her community.

Segomotsi's extensive background in local construction, combined with a deep passion for empowering communities, drives every project. She envisioned a firm that not only delivers top-notch construction but cultivates lasting relationships.

"Building for today, with an eye on tomorrow."

Agriculture & Third-Party Funding

FARMERS
OF FARMERS

Osenotse General Trading is restoring its farmers — not just to the land, but to their futures. When we help someone achieve their dream of becoming a farmer, we are committing to so much more than just arranging a profession or a plot of land to be cultivated.

A farm isn't just a business one starts. It's a long-term, multi-generational investment. What we are cultivating is an assured future for that farmer's family, for generations to come. Now, that's real progress!

We see ourselves as farmers of farmers — believing that restoring any society begins in restoring the farmer. Not just to the land, but to their future.
